### Google Play API 35 Requirement
<div style="background: #FAFAFA; border-left: 3px solid #EA580C; border-radius: 4px; padding: 1rem; margin: 1rem 0; color: #1F2937 !important;">

<span style="background: #EA580C; color: white; padding: 2px 8px; border-radius: 4px; font-size: 0.75rem; font-weight: 600;">HIGH</span> **Deadline: August 31, 2025**

Apps failing requirement will be blocked from submission/updates. Extension available until November 1.

**Impact:** Store submission, Build/Release pipeline

**Actions:**
- Set `targetSdkVersion=35`
- Update blocked dependencies (permissions/file access)
- Run Android 15 compatibility tests
  - Predictive back gesture
  - Behavior changes

**Test Matrix:** Android 15 (API 35)
</div>
